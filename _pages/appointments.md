Welcome! Please use the system below to request an appointment for office hours or research consultations.

### How it works:
1. Select your preferred day and time (Tue/Wed 17:00-18:00, Fri 16:00-18:00).
2. Fill in your details and submit the request.
3. **Wait for Confirmation**: You will receive an email once I have reviewed and approved your appointment. The slot will be automatically reserved once confirmed.
