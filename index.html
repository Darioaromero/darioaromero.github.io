--- layout: home author_profile: true header: image: "/images/Header.jpg" # caption: "Aljaferia Palace, Zaragoza, Spain" --- Dario Romero is a Economics Ph.D student at Columbia University in the City of New York. He holds a M.A. in Economics and B.A. in Economics and Political Science from Universidad de los Andes. His research interest is in political economy, development economics, and applied microeconomics. He has focused his research on the economic and political consequences of conflict and crime, and he has worked on evaluations for education policies in Latin America.
